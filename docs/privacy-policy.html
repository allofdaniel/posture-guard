Privacy Policy

Posture Guard - Posture Reminder App

1. Data Collection

  • Camera: Used as a mirror only to help you check your posture. Video is displayed locally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Usage Statistics: Session counts, monitoring times, and alert counts are stored locally on your device only.
  • Advertising: Google Mobile Ads may collect anonymized device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out in your device settings.

2. Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using secure storage.
  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud storage.
  • You can clear all app data anytime through your device settings.

3. Your Rights

  • Access, modify, or delete your data at any time.
  • Disable notifications in app settings.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads in your device's privacy settings.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob's privacy policy applies: Google Privacy Policy

5. Disclaimer

This app is NOT a medical device.

Posture Guard provides timed reminders only and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. The app uses the camera as a visual mirror - it does not analyze or measure your posture.

For any health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
